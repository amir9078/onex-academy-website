Our accreditation — KHDA-approved and internationally recognised

OneX Academy is the training and education provider. Our programmes carry multiple accreditations — Vocatech, a KHDA-approved training body in the UAE, plus additional international accreditation and recognition. This page explains exactly what that means, what it doesn't mean, and how to verify it, in plain language rather than marketing copy.

You train with OneX Academy, get certified through Vocatech and our international accreditation partners, then verify it yourself — every step here is real and checkable, not just claimed.

Who does what

It's a simple split, but worth spelling out clearly since a lot of academies blur it — OneX Academy designs and delivers the training, while our accreditation partners certify it. Neither one substitutes for the other.

  • OneX Academy designs and delivers the training — curriculum, instructors, the placement engine, the live hotel internship and the 6-month practical internship that follows every programme.
  • Vocatech accredits and certifies the programmes in the UAE, operating under KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) approval.
  • Additional international accreditation extends that recognition beyond the UAE — see below for what's confirmed and what's pending publication.

OneX Academy programmes are accredited by Vocatech, a KHDA-approved training body, plus additional international accreditation and recognition. That's the full relationship — we don't self-certify.

What the certificate means

Plain language, no overstatement

On completion of an accredited OneX Academy programme, you receive a certificate issued under our accreditation partners — KHDA-approved via Vocatech in the UAE, plus international accreditation and recognition. This is a professional certificate or short-programme credential — it is not a university degree, and we don't present it as equivalent to one.

For real estate specifically: this certificate is not the legally required RERA broker licence. RERA licensing is issued separately by the Dubai Land Department / DREI. OneX training is designed to complement the RERA licence, not replace it — see our Real Estate hub for how the two fit together.

If a specific programme or accreditation isn't confirmed at the time you enrol, we will say so plainly — it will be described as a “certificate of completion” rather than an accredited certificate until confirmed. The same honesty runs through everything else on this site — the 6-month internship, the placement engine and the add-ons are all real, current offerings, not aspirational claims.
