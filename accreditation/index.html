Trust & verification

Our accreditation — KHDA-approved certification via Vocatech

OneX Academy is the training and education provider. Programme accreditation and certification are provided by Vocatech, a KHDA-approved training body. This page explains exactly what that means, what it doesn't mean, and how to verify it.

Who does what

  • OneX Academy designs and delivers the training — curriculum, instructors, the placement engine and the live hotel internship.
  • Vocatech accredits and certifies the programmes, operating under KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) approval.

OneX Academy programmes are accredited by Vocatech, a KHDA-approved training body.

Permit / registration number

Vocatech's KHDA permit/registration number will be published here and on our certificates once confirmed for these programmes.

What the certificate means

Plain language, no overstatement

On completion of an accredited OneX Academy programme, you receive a KHDA-approved certificate issued under Vocatech accreditation. This is a professional certificate or short-programme credential — it is not a university degree, and we don't present it as equivalent to one.

For real estate specifically: this certificate is not the legally required RERA broker licence. RERA licensing is issued separately by the Dubai Land Department / DREI. OneX training is designed to complement the RERA licence, not replace it — see our Real Estate hub for how the two fit together.

If a specific programme is not yet accredited at the time you enrol, we will say so plainly — it will be described as a “certificate of completion” rather than a KHDA-approved certificate until accreditation is confirmed.
