The 15-day structure

You'll rotate through hotel departments and structured modules covering front-of-house, guest services, operations and the service-excellence standards a real Dubai hotel runs on — building a working understanding of how a hotel actually operates, not just how it's described in a textbook.

A full department-by-department, day-by-day schedule is confirmed at enrolment.

What's included

  • 15-day live hotel internship — real departments, real shifts
  • 15 structured modules — service excellence, operations, guest-facing skills
  • KHDA-approved certificate on completion
  • Recommendation letter from your internship
  • LinkedIn profile building — live before you finish
  • Placement support — guidance as you move into your first role

The hotel partner

Your internship takes place inside a working Dubai hotel partner — a 4/5-star property. The named partner is confirmed at enrolment, pending publication permission.

Career-changers with no prior hospitality experience, Dubai residents, and international applicants seeking a genuine internship, not an online-only course.
