About us

About OneX Academy

OneX Academy is Dubai's outcome-first training provider, built around one idea — a certificate should be the start of your placement, not the end of the course. We're an award-winning training provider trusted by 1500+ professionals across the UAE and India.

What we do

We deliver AI-integrated training across three areas — real estate, hospitality and professional AI skills. Every programme carries Vocatech's KHDA approval, plus additional international accreditation and recognition, and every programme is built around a real outcome — verified leads and brokerage placement for real estate, a live hotel internship for hospitality, practical applied skills across 8 AI Master Courses — followed by a 6-month practical internship on every path.

How we're different

  • Accredited and legitimate. KHDA-approved certification via Vocatech, with our permit number published on our accreditation page.
  • AI woven through every programme, not bolted on as a single module.
  • A real placement engine — verified leads and a dedicated Relationship Manager in real estate; a genuine, live hotel internship in hospitality; then a 6-month practical internship on every programme.
  • Built in Dubai, for the world — curriculum shaped by current RERA process and real hotel operations, but training you for a career with international reach, not just a local one.
