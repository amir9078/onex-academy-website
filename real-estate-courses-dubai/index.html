Where the RERA licence fits

We complement the licence, we don't replace it

The RERA broker licence is the legally required credential to practise as a real estate agent in Dubai, issued by the Dubai Land Department / DREI. OneX Academy does not replace it — we complement it.

Our programmes teach you the AI tools, lead generation and deal-closing skills the licence course doesn't cover, and our team can guide you toward sitting the official RERA exam. Think of the licence as your legal permission to operate, and OneX as what makes you good — and busy — once you have it.
