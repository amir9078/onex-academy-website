The Real Estate Intensive — four days, live in Dubai

Some agents learn fastest in a room with other people, not a browser tab. The Intensive takes everything in Career Launch and layers on 4 days of live, hands-on training in Dubai — deal clinics, negotiation role-play and real networking with working agents and brokerages — before continuing into a 6-month practical internship once you're certified.

Who it's for

  • Dubai residents who want to commute in for live, intensive training
  • International applicants who want full immersion — training plus an optional travel package to get here
  • Agents who learn faster from role-play and live feedback than from a screen

The 4-day agenda

  • Deal clinics — work real scenarios with experienced trainers, not hypotheticals
  • Role-play & negotiation practice — rehearse client and closing conversations before you need them live
  • Live networking — meet active agents, brokerages and OneX's placement network in person
  • Premium certificate and award — recognising the in-person commitment

Beyond the 4 days — a 6-month practical internship

The 4-day intensive isn't the end of your hands-on training — it's the start. Intensive graduates are eligible for a 6-month practical internship with a partner brokerage, carrying the deal-clinic reps and live coaching straight into real, paid work.
