Available add-ons

Eight ways to extend your training

Each add-on is built around a specific gap graduates told us they hit after certification — not generic upsells. Mix and match across programmes; several work well together.

L Real Estate

Extra Leads Pack

50 additional verified Dubai leads on top of what's already included in Career Launch or the In-Person Intensive — for agents who are closing through their first pack faster than expected and want to keep momentum going rather than wait for their next scheduled batch.

M Any programme

1:1 Mentorship & coaching

A per-hour personal coaching session with a working practitioner in your field — real estate, hospitality or AI. Use it to prep for a specific interview, work through a live deal or guest situation, or get direct feedback on how you're applying what you learned.

C Any programme

Premium certificate + LinkedIn badge

A printed, premium-finish version of your KHDA-approved certificate, plus a shareable LinkedIn credential badge you can add directly to your profile — useful the moment you start applying for roles or pitching yourself to a brokerage or hotel.

R Real Estate

CRM Pro upgrade

Six months of full CRM access beyond your programme's standard window — so the pipeline you built during training keeps running while you're out closing deals, not paused while you sort out your own tooling.

A Any programme

Alumni network + job board access

A full year of access to the OneX alumni network and job board — other graduates across real estate, hospitality and AI, plus roles posted directly by our placement and hiring partners as they come in.

P Hospitality

Placement support (Hospitality)

Extended placement assistance and introductions beyond what's included in the Hotel Career Immersion — more time with our placement team, more introductions, for graduates who want a longer runway to land the right role rather than the first one.

D Real Estate

Deal-closure accelerator

Three extra months of dedicated Relationship-Manager support, focused specifically on closing — for agents who want hands-on help pushing a specific deal, or several, over the line rather than general career coaching.

E Real Estate

RERA exam-prep crash bundle

Focused preparation for the official RERA broker licence exam — run alongside any Real Estate tier, so your Onex training and your legal licensing track move forward at the same time instead of one waiting on the other.
